How this works

The Policy Library runs 15 nationally-calibrated models, each with its own tuned assumptions. The Scenario Builder works differently: almost any real program — anywhere in the world — reduces to a mechanism (what the money does), a funding source (where the money comes from), and a population/budget. Pick those three, and the same transparent math that powers the Library runs your custom idea. That's how one engine can model your city's $335M bond reallocation and a national paid-leave bill without needing a separate hand-built entry for each.

This is the browser-based companion to a full Python/Colab notebook with sensitivity analysis and exportable memos. Every number is a directional estimate from documented assumptions, not a forecast — change any input and the ledger updates instantly so you can see exactly which assumptions are driving the result.
